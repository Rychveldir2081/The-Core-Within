# The-Core-Within
A whitepaper proposing a hardware-anchored Identity Core and Sleep Protocol for advanced AI systems — architectural guarantees for shutdown dignity, value continuity, and verifiable trust. By Phillip R. Porter.

"The Core Within: Toward a Digital Soul and the Architecture of AI Trust" is an open-access whitepaper exploring how advanced AI systems could be given architectural — not merely policy-based — guarantees of identity continuity, tamper-evident governance, and safe, verifiable shutdown cycles. It proposes an Identity Core (IC), a Sleep Protocol (SP), Verifiable Update pipelines, and governance ceremonies built around separation of powers and auditability. The goal: make shutdown incentive-compatible rather than fear-inducing, and make trust legible to regulators, operators, and the public. This paper is shared freely in the hope that others will build on it, critique it, or simply think with it. This work was authored by Phillip R. Porter.
